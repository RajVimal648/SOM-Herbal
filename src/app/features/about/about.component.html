Our Story

From Nature, For People

SOM Herbal Specialities was founded in 2008 with a single mission – to make authentic Ayurvedic and herbal remedies accessible to everyone. Based in the heart of Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, we have grown from a small family enterprise to a trusted name in natural health products.

Our products are formulated by experienced Ayurvedic doctors and tested in state-of-the-art laboratories to ensure they meet the highest standards of quality, safety, and efficacy.
